Introduction

We are delighted to present this concept and schematic design proposal for Ceremony of Roses' new workspace at 8439 Steller Drive. This proposal represents a thoughtful collaboration between interior design and architecture, bringing together our collective expertise to create a space that fosters creativity, collaboration, and growth.

Vision and Collaborative Approach

Our vision is to create a workspace that seamlessly blends functionality with aesthetic excellence. We believe in a highly collaborative approach that puts your team's needs at the forefront of every design decision. Through careful listening and iterative design, we will work together to create an environment that not only meets your current needs but also supports your future growth.

Commitment to Excellence

We are committed to delivering a project that exceeds expectations in both design quality and execution. Our team brings a wealth of experience in hospitality, residential, and commercial design, ensuring that every detail is considered and every space is thoughtfully crafted. We approach this project with the same level of care and attention that we bring to all our work.

Growth and Evolution

Version 2.0 An evolution of creative workspace, fostering the growth of your world-class team

Warm and Inviting Materials

We propose a material palette that emphasizes natural warmth with strategic color play, creating an environment that feels both professional and welcoming. Wood tones, natural textiles, and carefully selected finishes will create a foundation of comfort and sophistication.

Hospitality & Residential Touch

Drawing from our extensive hospitality experience, we'll infuse the workspace with client-informed function that prioritizes user experience. The space will feel more like a curated home than a traditional office, promoting creativity and well-being.

Lighting as Sculpture

Lighting will serve dual purposes as both functional illumination and sculptural art objects. Carefully selected fixtures will become focal points that define spaces and create atmosphere throughout the workspace.

Privacy and Openness

The design balances private rooms and open work spaces with an interplay of privacy and openness in between. Flexible zones will allow for collaboration while providing retreat spaces for focused work.

Depth of Experience

Our team brings decades of combined experience across hospitality, residential, and commercial projects. We have successfully delivered spaces that are not only beautiful but also highly functional and enduring.
